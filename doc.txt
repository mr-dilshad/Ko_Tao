2. Business Overview
The client operates a hostel cum scuba certification school in Koh Tao, Thailand. The business provides accommodation facilities along with SSI and/or PADI scuba diving courses for customers of different age groups and experience levels.
The objective is to create a strong online presence where customers can explore the property, view amenities, compare available scuba courses, understand SSI and PADI options, learn about the crew, and make online bookings for both courses and accommodation.
3. Business Objectives
Build a professional online presence for the hostel and scuba school.
Showcase SSI and PADI scuba courses in a clear and attractive manner.
Help users understand course levels, certification bodies, duration, pricing and eligibility.
Display hostel amenities, rooms, dormitories and facilities.
Enable online booking for scuba courses.
Enable online booking for dormitory / hostel stay.
Promote course + stay packages.
Increase direct bookings and reduce dependency on third-party platforms.
Improve customer engagement through rewards, coupons, blogs and marketplace features.
Build trust by showcasing experienced instructors, certifications, reviews and safety standards.
Create a scalable platform that can grow into a complete scuba travel ecosystem.

20. Final Client Summary
The proposed platform will help the client build a professional digital identity, showcase SSI and PADI scuba courses and hostel facilities, accept online bookings, engage customers through rewards and coupons, and gradually grow into a complete scuba travel and community platform for Koh Tao.
The recommended approach is to start with a focused POC or MVP, validate customer response, and then expand into advanced features such as marketplace, customer dashboard, referral system, mobile app and CRM automation.
