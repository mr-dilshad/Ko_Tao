SSI SCUBA DIVING COURSE CATALOG
For Hostel + Scuba Diving School Platform — Koh Tao

Note:
SSI course availability depends on the dive center’s authorization, instructor qualifications, local dive conditions, and business focus. For a hostel + scuba school website, the most commercially relevant courses are beginner scuba courses, advanced/specialty courses, rescue courses, professional courses, and selected technical/extended range programs.

============================================================
1. BEGINNER / ENTRY-LEVEL COURSES
============================================================

1. TRY SCUBA

Course Name:
Try Scuba

Description:
An introductory shallow-water scuba experience. It allows guests to try scuba diving in a safe and controlled environment before committing to a full certification course.

Who Is It For:
- Complete beginners
- Hostel guests
- Families
- Travelers unsure whether they want to take a full scuba course
- People who want a short first-time underwater experience

Time Required:
Usually a few hours, depending on the dive center schedule and local conditions.

Prerequisites:
- Basic comfort in water
- Medical/admin forms
- Instructor approval

Benefits:
- Good introduction to scuba diving
- Helps customers decide whether they want to continue into Basic Diver or Open Water Diver
- Strong conversion product for hostel guests
- Low time commitment

Reference Document:
SSI Try Scuba official course page:
https://www.divessi.com/en/get-certified/scuba-diving/try-scuba

------------------------------------------------------------

2. BASIC DIVER

Course Name:
Basic Diver

Description:
A short beginner scuba program covering basic dive skills in confined water and open water. It gives students more experience than Try Scuba but is shorter than the full Open Water Diver course.

Who Is It For:
- Beginners who want more than a trial dive
- Travelers with limited time
- Guests who may later continue to full certification

Time Required:
Approximately 10–12 hours.

Prerequisites:
- Minimum age: 10 years
- Basic water comfort
- Water fitness evaluation for entry-level programs
- Medical/admin forms

Benefits:
- Quick scuba learning experience
- Can act as a stepping stone toward Scuba Diver or Open Water Diver
- Good for customers who want a short structured program

Reference Document:
SSI Basic Diver official course page:
https://www.divessi.com/en/get-certified/scuba-diving/basic-diver

------------------------------------------------------------

3. SCUBA DIVER

Course Name:
Scuba Diver

Description:
A limited certification course that allows the student to dive in open water up to 12 meters / 40 feet under the direct supervision of an SSI Professional.

Who Is It For:
- Travelers with limited time
- Beginners who want a recognized certification
- Guests who cannot complete the full Open Water Diver course
- People who want to start diving but remain under professional supervision

Time Required:
Approximately 10–16 hours.

Prerequisites:
- Minimum age: 10 years
- Water fitness evaluation
- Medical/admin forms

Benefits:
- Faster route than Open Water Diver
- Recognized certification
- Can later be upgraded to Open Water Diver
- Good for short-stay travelers

Reference Document:
SSI Scuba Diver official course page:
https://www.divessi.com/en/get-certified/scuba-diving/scuba-diver

------------------------------------------------------------

4. OPEN WATER DIVER

Course Name:
Open Water Diver

Description:
The main full beginner scuba certification course. It trains students in scuba theory, confined-water skills, and open-water diving so they can become certified recreational divers.

Who Is It For:
- First-time divers who want full certification
- Backpackers and travelers staying several days
- Families and young learners
- People who want to dive independently within recreational limits
- Customers planning future diving trips

Time Required:
Approximately 16–32 hours.
Commonly completed in 3–4 days, depending on dive center schedule.

Prerequisites:
- Minimum age: 10 years
- Water fitness evaluation
- Medical/admin forms
- Basic swimming ability and comfort in water

Benefits:
- Full globally recognized beginner certification
- Main revenue-generating course for a dive school
- Enables customers to continue into advanced and specialty courses
- Builds confidence, safety, and basic dive independence

Reference Document:
SSI Open Water Diver official course page:
https://www.divessi.com/en/get-certified/scuba-diving/open-water-diver

------------------------------------------------------------

5. SCUBA SKILLS UPDATE

Course Name:
Scuba Skills Update

Description:
A refresher program designed to help certified divers review and practice scuba skills before returning to open-water diving.

Who Is It For:
- Certified divers who have not dived for a long time
- Nervous returning divers
- Travelers wanting a safe refresh before fun dives
- Guests who want to regain confidence

Time Required:
Usually half-day to one day, depending on the center.

Prerequisites:
- Previous scuba certification
- Medical/admin forms if required by the center

Benefits:
- Improves diver confidence
- Refreshes important safety skills
- Good add-on before fun dives or advanced courses
- Reduces risk for inactive divers

Reference Document:
SSI Scuba Diving course overview:
https://www.divessi.com/en/get-certified/scuba-diving
